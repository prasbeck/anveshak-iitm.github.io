Support Us

On our journey to excellence, we are determined to climb to a position amongst the top 10 in URC 2019. The team is working on research and development for exciting innovations in the field of robotics, the requirement for which is having cutting edge technology at our disposal.

While the team receives financial aid from the institute, Team Anveshak is seeking both financial and in-kind sponsorships to enable us to reach our goals that much more quicker. We value partnership with our sponsors. We would also be interested to work together with collaborative sponsors on research and development that would be mutually beneficial to our organizations. Team Anveshak requires assistance from sponsors for: Financial assistance for travel and logistics Electronic components like microprocessors, sensors and industrial grade connectors Raw material, fabrication and mechanical components such as bearings and conveyor belts Sophisticated equipment such as a workstation, cameras and sensors
