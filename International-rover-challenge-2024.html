International Rover Challenge (IRC)2024

The International Rover Challenge (IRC) 2023, conducted by SPace RObotics Society(SPROS), is scheduled to take place at PSG-iTech in Coimbatore, India. In order to demonstrate our capabilities, we will be undertaking a series of missions and tasks:

1.AutoExp

Witness the rover's independent thinking! Programmed to calculate the optimal route, the rover navigates with agility, guided by arrows on the field — a true marvel of autonomous exploration.

2.AbEx

The rover undergoes a transformation into a mobile science laboratory, carefully gathering and analyzing soil samples to reveal potential indicators of life.

3.IDMO

Testing the rover's dexterity, our robotic arm takes center stage. It interacts with a Maintenance Board, showcasing its ability to handle various tasks — from operating drawers and switches to keyboards and joysticks.

4.RDO

Navigating challenging terrains, our rover identifies, retrieves, and delivers specific objects to the base station.

5.PIMA

The team's efficiency in planning, execution, and the overall success of our project is evaluated.

6.BPP

Our business acumen takes the spotlight in BPP. We will be assessed on our strategic thinking and business mindset, proving that our vision extends beyond the rover's wheels!
