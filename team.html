Support Us

Team Anveshak comprises of 30+ enthusiastic undergraduates from different verticals of engineering working towards development of a semi-autonomous vehicle capable of extreme terrain traversal and scientific exploration. Our team brings together a set of highly dedicated & skilled students working on mechanical design, system architecture & control, outreach & business relations. We also organize educational workshops for students of our institute on different aspects of robotics. The technical knowledge & practical experience the team imparts to its members is unparalleled, nurturing them into effective team players & leaders of industry & research.
